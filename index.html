About

I’m a third-year Electronics and Computer Engineering student who enjoys building innovative solutions by observing real-world problems.

Often, simple moments—like looking out of a bus window—trigger ideas about how everyday systems can be improved through technology. I have a strong interest in embedded systems and IoT, and I enjoy working on projects driven by curiosity and practical impact.

One project I’m particularly proud of is a smart street light system that addressed a real social problem. Although the edge computing component is still a work in progress, the project deepened my interest in embedded and intelligent systems.

Currently, I’m exploring machine learning, hardware-oriented design, and IoT communication technologies such as LoRaWAN. My goal is to strengthen my foundation in edge computing while actively seeking internships in machine learning and embedded systems.
